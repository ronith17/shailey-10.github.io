How we go about it?

Take a deep dive into how we get you results.

#1 Defining

Talking with you is a huge part of how we create websites. Who are your ideal customers. What is it that you do different . What are the thing you are looking to improve through you website and your goals. This helps us get an idea of what you are looking for!

#2 Design

Once we have defined what you need we move onto design the website. We send you multiple designs with different colors and layouts. So that you have a variety of designs to mix and match from!

#3 Discussion

At various stages of the design phase mentioned in step two we will get to you for feedback and approval to know which styles you prefer. Your feedback is extremely important for as we will make amendments to the layout and design according to you!

#4 Develop

Once the design phase is complete I can then begin writing the actual code that will build your website for the web. We work on this step very professionally so as to ensure the visitor is directed to the right places and you get the best results from the site.

#5 Launch

Once development, testing, and final amendments are finalised, we handle the entire launch process to get your new website online and available to your online audience. We further help you run campaigns to genererate more traffic to your website if you wish to do that!

Once your website has launched we offer a maintenance program which is completely free for the first three months.
